### Key Clinical Implications

1. **Both HCC and ICC**: Segmental resection provides equivalent or better survival than larger resection in elderly
2. **ICC patients**: Benefit MORE from chemotherapy, LESS from transplant
3. **HCC patients**: Transplant is the strongest protective factor (HR ≈ 0.12)
4. **ICC surgery paradox**: Despite worse overall prognosis, surgery benefit magnitude is SIMILAR (surgery × ICC interaction NS)
5. **Cirrhosis**: Present in 25% HCC vs <5% ICC — major confounder for treatment selection
